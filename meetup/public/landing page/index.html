About

Get all your friends and family together in one place for a group video chat on Meetup. Meetings, chat, phone, webinars, and online events are all powered by Meetup's's secure, dependable video platform. It allows two users to video call simultaneously with additional feature of showing or stopping video, muting or unmuting audio. I used JavaScript, React, Socket.io, and WebRTC to create the webapp.

I built the webapp with React, and I used Socket.io and webRTC's simple Peer to connect the users. The video calling feature has been successfully implemented. The web app allows more than two people to video call together.
