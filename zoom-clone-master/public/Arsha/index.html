About

Get all your friends and family together in one place for a group video chat on Meetup. Meetings, chat, phone, webinars, and online events are all powered by Meetup's's secure, dependable video platform. It allows two users to video call simultaneously with additional feature of showing or stopping video, muting or unmuting audio

Multi-User Video Calling App using libraries like WebRTC, Socket.io and Node.js.

  • Video calls have become an essential component of modern communication.
  • Social video chat is about simplicity and interactivity,Wherever you go, stay connected.
  • Securely connect, collaborate and celebrate from anywhere.
  • Easy access on any device: Share a link and invite team members to join your conversations with one click
  • Quickly respond or react to threaded conversations with emojis
